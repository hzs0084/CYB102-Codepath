## WEEK 2

Host Intrusion Detection Systems (HIDSs) are very important in protecting systems from potential attacks by actively monitoring for possible threats. Cisco is a popular company that offer HIDS services, as well as Snort and Splunk which we'll take a look at in Units 3 and 5, respectively.

This unit is all about endpoint monitoring, or in other words - ways to monitor computers and other devices on a network to detect nefarious events, potential vulnerabilities, or even intrusions into your network! This means we will check out Windows and Linux event logs and how they can be used to track potential threats and problems. During our lab, we will get some experience using a Host Intrusion Detection system and will be tasked with trying to find the malicious actor who is attempting to break into a Windows machine! Our project will involve figuring out what configuration file was tampered with on the server!
